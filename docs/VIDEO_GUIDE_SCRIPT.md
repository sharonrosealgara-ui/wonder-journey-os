# 🎬 Wonder Journey OS — Navigation Video Guide (Canva-Ready Script)

A complete, scene-by-scene storyboard for a friendly "how to use the platform"
video, for **future client families**. It has two clear parts you can keep
together or export as two shorter videos:

- **Part A — For the Family & Students** (~90 sec)
- **Part B — For the Teacher** (~90 sec)

Each scene below gives you four things:
1. **⏱️ Duration** — rough seconds
2. **🎥 Show** — what to screen-record or screenshot
3. **💬 On-screen text** — type this into the Canva slide
4. **🎙️ Say (voiceover)** — read this aloud (or use Canva's Text-to-Speech)

---

## 🎨 Canva setup (do this once)

- **New design:** Video → 1920×1080 (or Mobile Video 1080×1920 if it's mainly for phones).
- **Brand colors** (paste these hex codes into Canva → Brand/Styles):
  - Sand cream `#FBF3E6` · Ocean teal `#0E7C86` · Mango `#FFB42E`
  - Sunset `#FF7D4D` · Hibiscus pink `#E5568A` · Palm green `#4DA66A` · Ube purple `#7F7AD1`
- **Fonts:** Headings **Fredoka** (or Baloo 2); body **Nunito**. Warm and rounded, like the app.
- **Music:** Canva → Audio → search "ukulele" or "playful acadia / island" — keep it soft, low volume under narration.
- **Transitions:** use the same one throughout (a gentle "Dissolve" or "Slide") so it feels calm, not busy.

### How to get the app footage
1. Open `https://wonder-journey-os.netlify.app/?guest=1` (guest link — no code needed).
2. Use your screen recorder (the one in your taskbar) to record short 5–10 sec clips of each page as you click through.
3. Drag those clips onto the matching Canva slides, OR just take screenshots and add arrows/zoom in Canva.

> 💡 Tip: record clips a little longer than you need — it's easier to trim in Canva than to re-record.

# 🎬 PART A — FOR THE FAMILY & STUDENTS

### Scene A0 — Warm welcome (title)
- ⏱️ 8 sec
- 🎥 Show: the watercolor Home Base painting / logo, or a title card with the 🧭 logo
- 💬 On-screen: **Welcome to Wonder Journey** · *Your family's learning adventure*
- 🎙️ Say: "Welcome to Wonder Journey — the cozy place where your whole family learns together. Let me show you around in just a couple of minutes."

### Scene A1 — Home Base
- ⏱️ 12 sec
- 🎥 Show: the Home Base page; hover the left sidebar, then point to the top bar (Explorer Level, Points ⭐, Stamps 🛂, Badges 🏅)
- 💬 On-screen: **🏠 Home Base — your launchpad**
- 🎙️ Say: "This is Home Base. On the left is your menu — every part of the adventure lives here. Up top you'll see your Explorer Level, points, passport stamps, and badges growing as you learn. Tap 'Begin Today's Adventure' to jump straight in."

### Scene A2 — Morning Blessings
- ⏱️ 12 sec
- 🎥 Show: the Morning Blessings page — the verse of the day and a family member writing in their journal card
- 💬 On-screen: **☀️ Morning Blessings — start the day together**
- 🎙️ Say: "Each morning begins with Morning Blessings — a verse of the day, and a little journal for everyone in the family to write what they're grateful for. It's gentle, never graded — just a warm start to the day."

### Scene A3 — Live Classroom
- ⏱️ 15 sec
- 🎥 Show: the Live Classroom — the join screen with the class code box, then the room with the two cameras (Family above Teacher)
- 💬 On-screen: **🎥 Live Classroom — see Teacher, together**
- 🎙️ Say: "The Live Classroom is where you meet your teacher face to face. Type in your class code once, and you're in — your family's camera on top, your teacher's below. You'll learn, chat, raise a hand, and explore the lesson right on the same screen."

### Scene A4 — Adventure Map & the lesson
- ⏱️ 14 sec
- 🎥 Show: Adventure Map (list of lessons), click one → the Adventure Theater opening, flip a couple of slides
- 💬 On-screen: **🗺️ Adventure Map — every lesson is a journey**
- 🎙️ Say: "The Adventure Map holds all your lessons. Open one and it becomes a storybook you flip through together — with pictures, new words, videos, and a mascot guiding the way. The scenery even changes with each part of the story."

### Scene A5 — Game Arcade (the fun part!)
- ⏱️ 14 sec
- 🎥 Show: the Game Arcade slide — the four game cards, then a quick clip of Fact Hunt or Memory Flip, and the "Pass & Play" siblings turn banner
- 💬 On-screen: **🎮 Game Arcade — learning that feels like play**
- 🎙️ Say: "Every lesson has a Game Arcade — Fact Hunt, Word Match, Memory Flip, and Word Scramble, all built from that day's lesson. Take turns as a family, beat your best score, and the games even adjust to each child's age. No one gets bored!"

### Scene A6 — Passport & Badges
- ⏱️ 10 sec
- 🎥 Show: the Passport page (stamps) then Badges page
- 💬 On-screen: **🛂 Passport & 🏅 Badges — collect your journey**
- 🎙️ Say: "As you finish adventures, you collect passport stamps for every place you visit and badges for every milestone — a keepsake of how far you've traveled."

### Scene A7 — Cooking Academy
- ⏱️ 10 sec
- 🎥 Show: Cooking Academy → open a recipe (Mango Float) with tap-to-check steps
- 💬 On-screen: **🧭 Cooking Academy — taste the culture**
- 🎙️ Say: "In the Cooking Academy you'll cook real dishes from the country you're exploring, step by step — then save your family's memory in the Cookbook."

### Scene A8 — Celebrations & Voice Messages
- ⏱️ 14 sec
- 🎥 Show: Celebrations page — the countdown, then the 🎙️ Birthday Voice Messages mic; tap it, record a short "Happy birthday!"
- 💬 On-screen: **🎉 Celebrations — send a voice gift**
- 🎙️ Say: "Celebrations remembers everyone's birthday. And here's a favorite: tap the microphone and record a little voice message for whoever's celebrating. On their special day, they'll open the app and hear all your loving voices waiting for them."

### Scene A9 — Quick tour of the rest
- ⏱️ 8 sec
- 🎥 Show: fast, friendly montage clicking Adventure Tree 🌳, Family Cookbook 📖, Family Journal 📚, Languages 💬, Resources 🎬
- 💬 On-screen: **…and so much more to explore**
- 🎙️ Say: "There's even more — your Adventure Tree of memories, the Family Cookbook and Journal, a Languages corner, and a Resources shelf. Wander and enjoy!"

# 🎬 PART B — FOR THE TEACHER

### Scene B0 — Teacher intro
- ⏱️ 8 sec
- 🎥 Show: title card, or the sidebar scrolled to the **Teacher Portal** section (Lesson Plans, Class Prep Email)
- 💬 On-screen: **👩‍🏫 The Teacher Portal**
- 🎙️ Say: "Now, teachers — here's your studio. Everything you need to plan, teach, and encourage lives in the Teacher Portal."

### Scene B1 — Lesson Plans
- ⏱️ 12 sec
- 🎥 Show: the Lesson Plans page (`/teacher`)
- 💬 On-screen: **📋 Lesson Plans — your whole curriculum**
- 🎙️ Say: "Lesson Plans lays out your entire journey — every lesson, its goals, materials, and activities, all prepared and ready to teach."

### Scene B2 — Class Prep Email
- ⏱️ 12 sec
- 🎥 Show: the Class Prep Email page (`/prep-email`) — the copy-ready email
- 💬 On-screen: **✉️ Class Prep Email — families arrive ready**
- 🎙️ Say: "Before each class, the Class Prep Email gives you a ready-to-send note for parents — what to bring and how to prepare — so every family shows up ready to learn."

### Scene B3 — Teaching live
- ⏱️ 16 sec
- 🎥 Show: Live Classroom in teacher mode — open the lesson on the stage, use the ✏️ whiteboard, show the toolbar, then "🏁 Finish Lesson" vs "📞 End Call"
- 💬 On-screen: **🎥 Teach live — lesson, cameras & tools in one place**
- 🎙️ Say: "When you teach, the lesson opens right on the classroom stage while everyone's cameras stay on. Draw on the whiteboard, share your screen, and track who's participating. 'Finish Lesson' wraps up the teaching but keeps the call going for a cozy chat — 'End Call' says goodbye for the day."

### Scene B4 — Encouraging each child
- ⏱️ 12 sec
- 🎥 Show: Morning Blessings in teacher mode — giving a ❤️ and writing an encouragement note; and the parent summary copy button in the lesson
- 💬 On-screen: **💛 Encourage & recap — effortlessly**
- 🎙️ Say: "You can send each child a heart and a note of encouragement on their journal, and copy a warm class summary for parents in one tap — so families always feel connected to the journey."

### Scene B5 — Getting a family started
- ⏱️ 14 sec
- 🎥 Show: a slide with the two link styles typed out clearly
- 💬 On-screen:
  - **Invite a family:** `wonder-journey-os.netlify.app/?code=YOURCODE`
  - **Just exploring:** `wonder-journey-os.netlify.app/?guest=1`
- 🎙️ Say: "Getting a family started is simple. Send them their magic link with the class code inside — they tap it once and they're set up forever, cameras and all. Or share the guest link so a new family can explore everything first, no code needed."

### Scene B6 — Closing
- ⏱️ 8 sec
- 🎥 Show: the watercolor painting / logo again, warm smile
- 💬 On-screen: **Wonder Journey OS** · *Learn together. Explore together. Grow together.*
- 🎙️ Say: "That's Wonder Journey — where every lesson becomes an adventure, and every adventure becomes a memory. We can't wait to explore with your family."
