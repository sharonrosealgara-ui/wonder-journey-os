# 🌺 Wonder Journey OS — Project Charter

**Product:** Wonder Journey (public brand) / Wonder Journey OS (engineering name — Decision 001)
**First Learning World:** 🇵🇭 Discover the Philippines: A Family Learning Adventure
**Tagline:** *Every lesson is an adventure. Every adventure becomes a memory.*
**Charter date:** July 7, 2026 · **First class:** Monday, July 13, 2026

## Vision

A warm, joyful platform where children and parents learn together — language, culture,
values, cooking, gratitude, and prayer — and where every lesson leaves behind a family
memory. Long-term: a reusable, white-label family learning platform and commercial SaaS
(ROADMAP.md Phases 8–9).

## Mission (this season)

Teach Shaun & Taylor's family — Rylee (12), Ezra (10), Asa (9), Selah (7) — Filipino
languages, values, and culture through Season 1, "Our Great Philippine Adventure,"
beginning July 13, 2026.

## Scope

**In scope now:** the shipped MVP (student/parent/teacher modes, blessings, prayer,
lessons, languages, passport, cooking, cookbook, journal, awards, celebrations,
prep email) plus Season 1 content generation per CURRICULUM_FRAMEWORK.md.

**Out of scope until their roadmap phase:** backend/auth, payments, AI assistants,
multi-family support, mascots, native apps.

## Success Definition

- Children are excited to begin each lesson
- Parents feel involved
- The teacher spends less time preparing
- Family memories are preserved
- The software is reusable for future families

## Constraints & Principles

- Classes are real and weekly — **never break the app on a class day**
- The family's faith preferences are requirements (Decisions 005–009)
- Configuration-first; white-label-ready; portfolio-quality (Decisions 020–021, 027)
